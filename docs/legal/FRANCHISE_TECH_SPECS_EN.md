# Technical Specifications: Franchise OS v1.0 (PC Master Ecosystem)

## 1. Overview
**Franchise OS** is the high-level management terminal designed for PC Master zone operators. It enables decentralized management of technical services, financial auditing, and network supervision within an assigned geographic territory.

## 2. Technical Specifications
- **Platforms:** Windows 10/11 (X64), Linux (Ubuntu 22.04+, Fedora, Arch).
- **Architecture:** Rust-based core for maximum speed and security.
- **Connectivity:** Low-latency encrypted link with the Quantum Brain (Central AI).
- **Security:** Biometric authentication and mandatory Hardware Key.

## 3. Core Modules
### A. Territorial Dashboard
- Real-time heat map with active technicians.
- Monitoring of critical tickets with an SLA of under 15 minutes.
- Customer satisfaction statistics filtered by zone.

### B. Sentinel Audit
- Access to session recordings (anonymized by AI) for dispute resolution.
- Detection of fraud or off-platform payments via behavior analysis.

### C. Finance & Escrow
- Management of territorial digital wallets.
- Automatic tax reports ready for export.
- Configuration of bonuses and penalties for the technician network.

## 4. Operational Requirements
- Stable internet connection (minimum 20Mbps).
- Recommended hardware: 16GB RAM, 8-core Processor.
